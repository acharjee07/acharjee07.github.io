Champion of IEEE Video and Image Processing CUP 2021

Our team Samaritan was announced the champion of IEEE Video and Image Processing CUP 2021. We developed a Machine Learning model to detect human body keypoints from infrared images. We received recognition from BUET for winning the competition. Special thanks to our supervisor Professor Ariful Haque and mentor Shahruk Hossain for their guidance.

Our team members, including Sanjay Acharjee, SK Nahin, and Aurick Das, worked extremely hard for several months to achieve this success. We presented our approach at the IEEE ICIP Conference 2021.
