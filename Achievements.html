Achievements

Champion of IEEE Video and Image Processing Cup 2021

Date: September, 2021

Our achievement was featured on our university's website, recognizing our participation and success. link

The 2021 VIP Cup challenge aimed to find computer vision-based solutions for in-bed pose estimation under the covers using labeled data from no-cover cases. This challenge enabled the development of in-bed behavior monitoring technologies for novel subjects and environments where no prior training data was available.

Location: Alaska, USA

Organizer: IEEE Signal Processing Society

First runner-up in Dl Sprint Bengali Automatic Speech Recognition Competition

Date: September, 2022

Bengali is the fifth most spoken native language worldwide, but little work had been done on Bengali Speech Transcription. Due to the large audience and extensive opportunities, there was significant business and educational interest in developing AI for Bengali ASR (Automatic Speech Recognition). The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at BUET, in partnership with Bengali.AI presented the very first Bengali ASR competition, DL Sprint , as part of BUET CSE Fest 2022.

Location: Dhaka, Bangladesh

Award: First Runner-up

Organizer: BUET CSE Department & Bengali.AI Community

Finalist in Robi Datathon 2.0 out of 358 teams (Among top 10)

Date: June 2022

The Robi Datathon, known as the biggest platform for the growing Data Scientist, Data Engineer, and Data Analysts community, showcased their skills in a competitive format. It was the second edition of this flagship event. The Datathon provided an intense 24-36 hour competition where top talents, data scientists, and enthusiasts from Bangladesh had the opportunity to demonstrate the impact of their data-driven insights and business solutions on the lives of citizens. Contestants were required to apply their data modeling and business acumen skills to solve problem statements and propose their own models. The challenge involved building predictive models and developing business solutions based on the provided data. As a final contestant, I had the opportunity to participate in this exciting event.

Location: Dhaka, Bangladesh

Organizer: Robi Axiata Limited
