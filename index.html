About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Civil Engineering at the University of Texas at Arlington, where I serve as a Graduate Research Assistant at the iSET Lab under the guidance of Dr. Md Nazmus Sakib.

My research lies at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Civil Engineering, with a focus on leveraging Generative AI, Computer Vision, and Deep Learning to revolutionize construction management and structural health monitoring. I am particularly passionate about developing intelligent systems that enhance workplace safety and improve infrastructure sustainability.
