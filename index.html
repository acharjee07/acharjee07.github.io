About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Civil Engineering at the University of Texas at Arlington, where I serve as a Graduate Research Assistant at the iSET Lab under the guidance of Dr. Md Nazmus Sakib.

My research lies at the dynamic intersection of Artificial Intelligence, robotics, and civil engineering, where I'm dedicated to pioneering innovative solutions for the built environment. I leverage cutting-edge AI and robotics to completely revolutionize construction processes, making them safer, more efficient, and more sustainable. A core focus of my work involves utilizing advanced robotic sensing technologies to continuously monitor construction sites and infrastructure, providing real-time insights that not only enhance worker safety but also ensure the long-term health and integrity of our built world. My passion is to create intelligent systems that are shaping the future of infrastructure, making it smarter and more resilient for generations to come.
